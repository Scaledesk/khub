OUR PROCESS

Our streamlined process helps us deliver top-notch quality in minimum time at affordable prices.

Schedule a Free Meeting

01

Our interior design experts will meet you to know more about your wants, needs and unspoken requirements for your house.
We give free consultation in the initial meeting. Our experts will understand your requriements.

Site Layout & Conceptualization

02

With a series of site analysis and architectural drawings, our design team develope a lay out of the space. We design as per client requirements.
Each space has its own story to tell, so we design the space according to the story and built up a concept for the spaces.

Design Selection

03

The client is given 3 concepts and ideas to choose from, modifications are made in the selected option as per the choice of the client. A variety of mood boards and layouts are provided as well , giving the client a wide pallet to choose from.

Approval & Deployment

04

Get complete mood baord along with final layouts.Along with the Bill of quantity.Client's approval is taken at each step.
We then present to you a tailor made design for your space and start instruct out team to make the drawings into your dream reality.
